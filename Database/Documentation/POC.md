# Proof of Concept (POC)

## Problem Statement

Citizens can access government budget allocation data, but they often cannot verify whether sanctioned projects were completed, delayed, abandoned, or exceeded their allocated budgets.

This lack of transparency reduces public trust and makes it difficult to monitor government spending effectively.

---

## Current Challenges

* Budget information is scattered across multiple sources.
* Citizens cannot easily track project progress.
* Delayed and over-budget projects are difficult to identify.
* Complaint and audit information is not centralized.
* Limited public visibility into project execution.

---

## Proposed Solution

Public Budget Tracker is a platform that helps citizens monitor government-funded projects by combining:

* Project information
* Budget allocation details
* Audit records
* Citizen complaints
* Project status tracking

The system provides a unified dashboard and chatbot interface for easy access to project information.

---

## Key Features

### Project Tracking

Monitor project progress and completion status.

### Budget Monitoring

Compare allocated budget with actual expenditure.

### Complaint Tracking

View and manage citizen-reported issues.

### Audit Monitoring

Identify projects with budget variances and audit flags.

### AI Chatbot

Allow users to ask questions about projects and budgets.

---

## Expected Impact

* Increased transparency
* Improved citizen engagement
* Better accountability
* Faster identification of delayed projects
* Easier access to public spending information
